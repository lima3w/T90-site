---
title: "BSA Forms"
description: "Official Scouting America forms required for participation."
weight: 1
---

- [Annual Health and Medical Record](https://www.scouting.org/health-and-safety/ahmr/) — Required for all Scouts. Parts A & B required for every activity; Part C required for high-adventure activities and must be completed by a physician.
- [Unit Permission Slip](https://filestore.scouting.org/filestore/pdf/19-673.pdf) — Standard permission slip for troop outings and activities.
