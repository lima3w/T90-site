## History

BSA Troop 90 was founded in **1939** and has been continuously chartered by the **Centralia Rotary Club** ever since. Over our more than 85 years of operation, Troop 90 has proudly developed more than **120 Eagle Scouts** — a testament to the dedication of our Scouts, families, and volunteer leaders.

## Our Mission

We follow the mission of the Boy Scouts of America: to prepare young people to make ethical and moral choices over their lifetimes by instilling in them the values of the Scout Oath and Law. Troop 90 pursues that mission through weekly meetings, year-round camping, community service, and merit badge achievement.

## Charter Organization

Troop 90 is chartered by the **Centralia Rotary Club**, whose support has been the foundation of our troop for generations.
