Welcome to Troop 90

BSA Troop 90 has served the youth of Centralia, Missouri since 1939, chartered by the Centralia Rotary Club. Over our history we have produced more than 120 Eagle Scouts. We meet weekly and run a full year of camping, service projects, merit badge work, and high-adventure activities.
